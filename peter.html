Peter Parker

Peter Benjamin Parker gained his superhuman spider-powers and abilities after a bite from a radioactive spider at age fifteen.
Opting to maintain a secret identity, he employed his newfound abilities and crafted web gadgets to protect New York.
Eight years deep into his superhero journey, Peter is a seasoned and adept crime fighter but frequently grapples with balancing his superhero duties and personal life.
